What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

SubTally is built for one job: showing you what you're actually paying for each month — clearly, calmly, and without phoning home.

One hero total

Your monthly and annual spend, normalised across weekly, monthly, quarterly and yearly cycles — right at the top.

Sorted by renewal

Active subscriptions are ordered by what's coming next, so the bill that matters tomorrow is at the top.

Local reminders

Get nudged the day before a renewal, scheduled directly on your phone. No push servers, no notification middleman.

Trials & pauses

Mark anything as a free trial or temporarily paused. Those don't roll into your monthly total — only what you're actually paying.

Made to match

Light, dark or automatic, paired with seven accent colours — teal, blue, indigo, purple, rose, orange, green.

Export, anytime

Send your full list out as a CSV through the system share sheet — to email, Files, anywhere. Your data, your move.

The catalog

The usual suspects come pre-built with their brand colour and emoji. Anything missing? Add a custom service in a few seconds.
